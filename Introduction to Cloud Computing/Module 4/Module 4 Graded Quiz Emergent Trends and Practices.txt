1.Which of the following options describes a development principle, or action, for cloud-native applications?
  Correct Answer: Containerize the microservices for maximum flexibility, scalability, and portability.

2.Which of the following statements describes the DevOps approach and process?
  Correct Answer: A collaborative approach where business owners and development, operations, and quality assurance teams     collaborate to deliver software continuously.

3.Which of the provided options encapsulates the complete application modernization journey?
  Correct Answer: The journey from monolithic and service-oriented architecture to microservices architecture, from     physical servers and VMs to cloud infrastructure, and from the waterfall and agile methodology to DevOps.

4.How would a global flower delivery company leverage the Hybrid Multicloud architecture to meet its requirement of varying   capacity demands across different geographies?
  Correct Answer: By distributing their delivery service application across multiple cloud environments and providers     spread across multiple geographies.

5.Which of the following statements describes the DevOps approach and process?
  Correct Answer: DevOps process is a collaborative approach that defines how people build, deploy, and manage applications   in a cloud-native environment to deliver software continuously.

6.Which of these scenarios is NOT a good use case for serverless architecture?
  Correct Answer: Workloads characterized by long-running processes.

7.How can the microservices architecture approach be characterized?
  Correct Answer: Decomposition of large applications into core functions to construct a comprehensive and functional     application.

8.Fill in the blank. Microservices architecture is an approach in which a ______________ comprises many loosely coupled and   independently deployable smaller components or services.
  Correct Answer: Single application.

9.What does a cloud-native application consist of?
  Correct Answer: Microservices.

10.DevOps and Cloud share a _________ relationship.
   Correct Answer: Symbiotic.
