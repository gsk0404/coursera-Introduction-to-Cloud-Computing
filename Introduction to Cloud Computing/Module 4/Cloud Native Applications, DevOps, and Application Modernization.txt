1.Which of the following is a development principle for cloud-native applications?
  Correct Answer: Containerize the microservices for maximum flexibility, scalability, and portability.

2.Which of the following statements describes the DevOps approach and process?
  Correct Answer: A collaborative approach where business owners and development, operations, and quality assurance teams     collaborate to deliver software continuously.

3.Which of the provided options encapsulates the complete application modernization journey?
  Correct Answer: The journey from monolithic and service-oriented architecture to microservices architecture, from     physical servers and VMs to cloud infrastructure, and from the waterfall and agile methodology to DevOps.
